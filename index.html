About Dr. Quinn

Dr. JJ Quinn is licensed as a Doctor of Oriental Medicine (DOM), a Licensed Acupuncturist (LAc), and a Licensed Massage Therapist (LMT) in Arizona and New Mexico. She earned her M.S.O.M. from Southwest Acupuncture College in Santa Fe, NM, which is one of the Top 10 ranked schools in the country.

Nationally, Dr. Quinn is certified as a Diplomate in Oriental Medicine (Dipl OM), which signifies her expertise as much more than an acupuncturist. Her additional specializations in Eastern herbology and internal medicine combine gracefully with a background in Western anatomy and physiology to give her patients a clinical perspective that works.

Because of her continued passion for “nature as medicine” and for creating effective resources for her patients, Dr. Quinn is currently completing certification as an Eating Psychology Coach through the world’s leading mind-body nutrition education, The Institute for the Psychology of Eating. She is also a Certified Metabolic Typing Advisor (CMTA) and Functional Diagnostic Nutritionist (FDN).

Dr. Quinn has committed her life to creating "outside-the-bottle" healing opportunities for all. Eighteen years ago, she began her professional career by teaching health and biomechanics curriculum at Rainstar College of Natural Therapeutics while concurrently completing her Master Graduate training in Massage and Craniosacral Therapy. Her subsequent years as a healer have been devoted to the mission of reclaiming REAL HEALTH for her clients. She is a true pioneer in the Valley’s alternative healthcare field.
