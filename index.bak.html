About Dr. Quinn

Dr. Quinn earned a Master of Science in Oriental Medicine (MSOM) from Southwest Acupuncture College, one of the oldest and most respected schools of Acupuncture and Asian Holistic Medicine in the United States. She is a licensed as a Doctor of Oriental Medicine (DOM), Licensed Acupuncturist (LAc), and Licensed Massage Therapist (LMT) in Arizona and New Mexico.

Nationally, Dr. Quinn is a certified Diplomate in Oriental Medicine (Dipl OM), which signifies her specialization in various forms of Asian Medicine: acupuncture, Chinese herbology, and the biomedical integration of eastern with current western medicine.

More recently, Dr. Quinn continued her passion for understanding "Food as Medicine" for her clients by becoming a Certified Metabolic Typing Advisor (CMTA) and Functional Diagnostic Nutritionist (FDN).

Dr. Quinn began her career by teaching the health and biomechanics curricula at Rainstar College of Natural Therapeutics, while concurrently completing her Master Graduate training in Massage and Craniosacral Therapy.

Dr. Quinn has committed her life to learning "outside-the-bottle" healing therapies. Fourteen years ago she helped herself and began a mission of reclaiming REAL HEALTH for her clients. She is a pioneer in the Scottsdale alternative healthcare field.
